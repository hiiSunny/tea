About masala tea

Masala chai literally ("mixed-spice tea") is a flavoured tea beverage made by brewing black tea with a mixture of aromatic Indian spices andherbs. Originating in the Indian subcontinent, the beverage has gained worldwide popularity, becoming a feature in many coffee and tea houses.

Reason why i like it so much.

No matter what season, a sip from a hot cup of chai is all you need to kick start your day on a positive note. As you travel throughout India, one thing that is sure to welcome you at every corner is a chaiwallah (tea maker). With huge pots of steaming milk and spices, they present you with various versions of the masala chai that melts away all the fatigue and stress.
